TOPIC
	about_PowerZwift
	
SHORT DESCRIPTION
	An automation module for the Zwift online cycling game, and third party add-ons
	
LONG DESCRIPTION
	Zwift Power Users might appreciate this module, which allows you to automate things like installation, toggling music on and off, course selection, and numerous other actions

	For more, try 

	Get-Command -module PowerZwift

	then

	Get-Help <command name>
